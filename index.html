About This Portal
Context & Responsibilities

This portal centralizes all reporting for the Guest Concierge Area in Colombia — Cartagena and Medellín. The reports here are part of a formal performance tracking initiative launched in 2026, covering two core management responsibilities I've taken on as part of my role.

01
Concierge Area KPI Monitoring
Define, track, and report on Concierge KPIs

Responsible for establishing and maintaining the KPI framework for the Guest Concierge Area — defining what gets measured, collecting the data, and producing monthly and quarterly reports for management visibility.

KPI 1 · Revenue & Services KPI 2 · Itinerary Delivery KPI 3 · Client Satisfaction KPI 4 · Execution Quality KPI 5 · Billing Timeliness
Where to find it: The KPI Reports tab — monthly and quarterly.
02
Monthly Concierge Area Report
Operational reporting + continuous improvement

Responsible for preparing a monthly operational summary covering groups served, KPI highlights, client feedback, and project updates — for management visibility. This report also integrates a third responsibility: periodic review of initiatives, analysis of results and feedback, and implementation of improvement plans over time — especially as they relate to the concierge KPIs.

Groups served KPI highlights Client feedback Project updates Improvement plans
Where to find it: The Concierge Reports tab — monthly.

  • · Executive summary + conclusions
Concierge Report (monthly)
  • · Groups served that month
  • · KPI performance highlights
  • · Client feedback & satisfaction notes
  • · Operational updates by city
  • · Active projects & initiatives
  • · Improvement actions implemented
